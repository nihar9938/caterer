About Us

IT STARTED, QUITE SIMPLY, LIKE THIS...

Meal plans by AP catering and Meal Services is an affordable one stop solution for your everyday homemade food craving. We are the modern day Dabbawala, providing tiffin through an online platform in the cosmopolitan city of Bhubaneswar.

With an option to choose from various customisable meal plans, we bring to you the ability to have authentic homemade food from homemakers around you. All the meals are 100 % additive free and are prepared with the freshest ingredients in a home kitchen just like yours..

We take the best care of everything from picking the fresh vegetables, adding the right nutritional value to keeping the authenticity of traditional taste intact to delivering the food to you on-time. We make use of minimal oil and spices, yet make the food finger-licking. Relish the delicious taste of homemade Odia food away from your home with us.
